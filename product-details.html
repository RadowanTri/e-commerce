PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

This dress showcases Kin's simple and modern design aesthetic. It's made from cotton jersey and has a longline relaxed cut that falls to the ankle with gathered back detail and a neat V-neckline. Great with sandals, trainers or heels.

By buying cotton products from John Lewis & Partners, you're supporting more sustainable cotton farming through the Better Cotton Initiative.

BCI is a not-for-profit organisation which exists to make global cotton production better for the people who produce it, better for the environment it grows in, and better for the sector's future. BCI works with partners around the world such as WWF, Solidaridad and Cotton Connect to train farmers to produce cotton more sustainably.

Better Cotton is sourced via a principle of "mass balance" and is not physically traceable to end products. However, BCI Farmers benefit from the demand for Better Cotton in equivalent volumes to those we source.
